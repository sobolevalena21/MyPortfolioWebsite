Donate to reduce severe suffering of stray pets in Kyrgyzstan

Kind Hands, Inc.

About a year ago, I discovered a tiny organization Kind Hands KG in my home country of Kyrgyzstan (also known as "KG"), and it absolutely conquered my heart like no other! It rescues local stray pets and encourages the KG government to use humane ways to reduce their population in the streets.

Unlike developed countries, Kyrgyzstan does not have free sterilization programs, nor does it have even one pet shelter! City streets are flooded with animals who so often get run over by cars. Blooded, they are slowly dying on the side of the streets unless local people, often kids! attempt to help them.

I was regularly donating to the organization but realized I can help much more if I would open organization's branch in the U.S. Due to limitations of the KG banking system, international platforms like PayPal and GoFundMe are only a dream for KG charities.

And here I am, being a president of the U.S. nonprofit. My goal is to raise $800/month through the US branch. The buying power of the US dollar is high in KG, so even your smallest monthly contribution will make quite a difference.

  • Average cost of pet surgery in KG$150
  • Average cost for pet foster home in KG$35/month
  • Current medical debt of the organization$1,000
