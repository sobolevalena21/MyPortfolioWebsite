My Experience

Co-founder Honorlock Inc.

An edtech company my friend and I started back in 2014. Born out of a small idea for our school's business plan competition, it grew into a leading provider of the testing integrity solutions for the universities and corporations in the U.S. and abroad.

Financial Controller

Setting up and leading Honorlock's financial activities from 2014 to 2020. Accounting and Payroll in Quickbooks; Complex budgeting models in Excel and Google Sheets; Management of state taxes in most of the U.S. states.

Business Process Outsourcing

Opening a satellite office for Honorlock in my home country Kyrgyzstan, which allowed the company to maintain 24/7 tech support at affordable costs.

Edtech Conferences

Managing company’s participation at edtech conferences: organizing travel, designing and ordering booth setup and marketing materials.

Mentorship

Writing and speaking for professional organizations and media in Kyrgyzstan and mentoring Kyrgyzstani students.

Charity Work

Helping a Kyrgyzstani stray pet organization to set up their donation channels in the U.S.

Web Development

Completed Full-Stack Engineer Course on Codecademy.
